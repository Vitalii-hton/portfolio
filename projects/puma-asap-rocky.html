Rigger & Animator & Technical artist

Challenge
The project required a comprehensive rigging solution for both vehicles (C4D/Maya) and characters, with a pipeline designed for rapid and accurate asset replacement to facilitate director approvals.

Impact
Developed rigs (C4D/Maya) and structured the scene pipeline to enable the non-destructive replacement of assets while fully preserving animation data. This process reduced visual iteration time and ensured flexibility in finalizing the director's desired look.
